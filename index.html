About

TypeTuned is an AI-powered writing tool designed to help you communicate with precision and intention. Whether you're sending a casual message, writing something professional, or navigating sensitive conversations, TypeTuned helps you strike the right tone. With a clean, intuitive interface and real-time rewriting, it’s like having a personal editor that instantly understands what you’re trying to say — and says it better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people express themselves more clearly, confidently, and authentically. In a world where communication can make or break opportunities, we believe everyone deserves the tools to speak with clarity and impact. TypeTuned empowers users with tone-aware rewrites, bridging the gap between what you feel and how you say it.
